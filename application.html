Cookie Clicker

How Cookie Clicker Works

Cookie Clicker is a fun and addictive incremental game where the goal is to bake as many cookies as possible. Here's how it works:

  1. Click on the big cookie to bake cookies.
  2. Use the cookies you bake to buy upgrades and buildings that automatically produce more cookies.
  3. Keep clicking and upgrading to increase your cookie production rate.
  4. Watch your cookie count grow exponentially as you progress!
